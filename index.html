OSRS Jewellery XP/GP Calculator

What is the purpose of the website?

To help calculate gold cost, profit and xp gain generated from crafting different free to play jewellery.

What do you want the website to accomplish?

I wish for it to have a calculator that allows you to input how many of what you want to make, and it to take from live grand exchange prices to generate a profit. Additionally it would show xp gain from the process.

Describe what needs to happen for you to consider your website a success.

The calculator has to work, and give useful information that can be relied on in-game.

Who is your target audience?

Oldschool RuneScape players.

What opportunity or issue is your website addressing?

This website would aid in the need to manually calculate what you want to make and help quicker determine if it is worth crafting specific pieces of jewellery for xp or profit.

What type of content might be included in your website?

Life grand exchange prices of gold, free to play gemstones, and crafting experience. Another useful thing would be an estimated time it would take a player to craft all their items; at peak performance, average performance, and "afk" performance.
